Dress the Avataris an interactive dress-up interface where users can style a digital avatar by selecting tops, bottoms, shoes, and accessories. Clothing appears on the avatar when clicked, and clicking the item again removes it and sends it back to its original position.The project combines layered PNG assets, UI design, and JavaScript-driven interactions to create a simple, playful outfit-building tool. The experience mimics the dress-up games many of us played growing up, but reimagined with a cleaner, contemporary layout and intentional interface design.
